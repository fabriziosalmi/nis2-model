# Terms of Use

**Last updated:** April 2026

## 1. Nature of the Service

The nis2-model ("the Tool") is an open-source software that provides **automated, preliminary analysis** of potential NIS2 Directive applicability based on user-provided parameters.

## 2. Not Legal Advice

> **The Tool does NOT provide legal advice.** All outputs — including applicability classifications, obligation listings, sanction calculations, and compliance reports — are generated by deterministic software logic and represent the Tool's automated classification, not a legal opinion.

The Tool's outputs:

- **Do not** constitute legal, regulatory, or professional advice of any kind
- **Do not** replace consultation with a qualified legal professional
- **Should not** be relied upon as the sole basis for compliance decisions
- **May not** reflect all applicable exceptions, national transpositions, or sector-specific rules

## 3. Limitations

The Tool evaluates a **subset** of the NIS2 Directive (EU 2022/2555):

- **Covered:** Articles 2, 3, 20, 21, 23, 34 (applicability, obligations, sanctions, incident reporting)
- **Not covered:** Articles 7-9, 13-14, 26-29, 32-33, 35 and other provisions
- **Not covered:** National transposition laws (e.g. D.Lgs. 138/2024 in Italy) — only the EU Directive
- **Simplified:** Size thresholds use a simplified model; linked/partner enterprise rules per EU Recommendation 2003/361/EC are not implemented
- **Simplified:** Sub-sector classification is accepted but not used in the decision logic
