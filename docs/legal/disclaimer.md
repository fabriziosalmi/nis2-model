# Legal Disclaimer

## Automated Analysis Tool — Not Legal Advice

The NIS2 Compliance Engine is a **software tool** that performs automated classification based on deterministic rules. It is **not** a law firm, legal advisor, or substitute for qualified legal counsel.

### What this tool does

- Applies a simplified model of NIS2 Directive (EU 2022/2555) Articles 2, 3, 20, 21, 23, and 34
- Classifies company profiles based on sector codes and size thresholds
- Generates structured reports from the classification output
- All outputs are deterministic: same input always produces same output

### What this tool does NOT do

- Provide legal advice or legal opinions
- Account for all exceptions in Art. 2(2)-(4) of the NIS2 Directive
- Implement national transposition laws (e.g. Italian D.Lgs. 138/2024)
- Evaluate sub-sector-specific rules or linked enterprise calculations
- Replace the judgment of a qualified legal professional
- Guarantee accuracy or completeness of its classifications

### Liability

The authors and contributors accept **no liability** for decisions made based on this tool's outputs. Users must independently verify all classifications with competent legal counsel before relying on them for compliance purposes.
